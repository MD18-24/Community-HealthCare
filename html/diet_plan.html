DEIT PlAN FOR INDIAN FAMILY

Breakfast:

  • Idli with sambhar and coconut chutney + a glass of fresh orange juice.
  • Poha with peanuts and coriander + a glass of buttermilk.
  • Oats porridge with chopped fruits and nuts + green tea.
  • Boiled eggs with whole wheat toast and a glass of milk.
  • Scrambled eggs with multigrain bread + a glass of fresh fruit juice.
  • Omelette with vegetables, whole wheat bread + herbal tea.

Lunch:

  • Mixed vegetable curry, dal tadka, steamed rice, and cucumber salad.
  • Rajma chawal (kidney beans with rice) and a side of carrot-beetroot salad.
  • Baingan bharta with bajra roti and onion-tomato salad.
  • Chicken curry with steamed rice and green salad.
  • Mutton biryani with cucumber raita and onion rings.
  • Prawn curry with rice and a side of green beans stir-fry.

Snack:

  • Roasted chana or a handful of nuts with masala tea.
  • A bowl of fresh fruits or a fruit smoothie.
  • Corn chaat with lemon juice and spices.
  • Grilled chicken skewers or boiled eggs.
  • A bowl of boiled chickpeas or sprouts with lemon and spices.
  • Roasted peanuts or boiled eggs with masala.

Dinner:

  • Palak paneer with roti and mixed vegetable raita.
  • Stuffed paratha (paneer or potato) with mint chutney and curd.
  • Matar paneer with jeera rice and a bowl of curd.
  • Fish curry with roti and sautéed vegetables.
  • Tandoori chicken with naan and a bowl of curd.
  • Egg curry with paratha and green salad.
