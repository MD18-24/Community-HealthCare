Healthy & Balanced Diet

Need of Healthy Diet

A healthy and balanced diet provides essential nutrients, supports growth, boosts immunity, and helps prevent diseases. It ensures overall physical and mental well-being.

Carbohydrates

Primary energy source for the body. Helps brain function and physical activity.

Need: 225–325g (45–65%)

Proteins

Builds and repairs tissues, supports immunity and muscle growth.

Need: 50–175g (10–35%)

Fats

Provide energy, protect organs, and help absorb vitamins.

Need: 44–77g (20–35%)

Minerals

Support bones, blood, muscles, and overall body functions.

Need: ~2800 mg
